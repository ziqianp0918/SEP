The goals of the program are: (1) to increase the number of high school graduates, particularly from traditionally under-represented groups, who are ready to enter new and emerging high-tech fields, and (2) to develop studentsâ€™ computational thinking and problem solving skills in real-world contexts.

Grades Breakdown

50% Assessments

  • Meet Deadlines on Assigments such as Projects & Portfolio's
  • Meet all Requirements listed on slides

  • "Done Is Better Than Perfect" -Mr.Mueller

30% Classwork Particpation

  • Ask Questions in Class
  • Even if you don't think you have the right answer, always participate
  • Be on time to class and always be on task

  • "There is no such thing as a dumb question" -Mr.Mueller

20% Homework

  • Put effort into homework assigments, don't rush to finish, put quality into the work
  • Sign up for the Remind system, it sends a text message to your phone reminding you of homework assigments so that you never forget
  • Also check the slides for any homework assignments

  • "You don't really get a lot of homework in this class, but when you do, its important that you do it" -Mr.Mueller
